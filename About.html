About the original owner

SSG. Fredrick Davis

This shop hasn't always been virtual. Up until recently, the Delicious Donut Depot was owned by

a retired US Army Staff Sergeant named Fred Davis. He ran the shop single-handedly in a small

town in Georgia. Once he decided that he was too old to be slinging the best donuts in the

world, he knew it was time to pass his business on to a fellow Veteran. SSG. Davis gave me his

shop under the stipulation that I bring it into the virtual world.


So here we are...
